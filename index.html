Features

Semantic search with synonym expansion, stemming, and TF-IDF ranking. Finds posts even when you don't remember the exact words.

Bookmark Posts

Pin important announcements and assignments. Your bookmarks stay synced and accessible from the sidebar in every class.

Deep PDF Search

Searches inside PDF attachments across all posts. Find that one line from a 50-page document in seconds.

🎯

Quickly filter between stream posts, assignments, and materials. Cut through the noise to find exactly what you need.

Easy on the eyes during late-night study sessions. Toggle dark mode from the extension popup with one click.

No setup needed. Install and the search bar appears on every class page. Lightweight with zero impact on performance.

1

Install

Add Classroom Plus from the Chrome Web Store. It's free, no account needed.

2

3

Your data stays yours

We only request the minimum permissions needed for each feature to work.

  • 📚
    Classroom Read Access Read your course list, announcements, assignments, and materials so the search feature can find posts across all your classes.
  • 📁
    Drive Read Access Read-only access to files attached to your Classroom posts. Used exclusively for Deep PDF Search to extract and search text inside documents.
  • 💾
    Local Storage Stores your bookmarks and search cache locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers.
